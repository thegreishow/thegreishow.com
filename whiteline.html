How it works

Simple enough to move quickly. Professional enough to trust.

White Line reviews submissions before adding talent to its active network. Clients receive a curated response based on availability, fit, budget, and production needs.

1

Send the right details

Talent submits a profile and portfolio links. Clients submit a brief, date, location, budget, and talent needs.

2

White Line reviews the request

Submissions are checked for professionalism, fit, availability, and the information needed to move forward.

3

Terms are confirmed

Scope, rates, commission, transportation, usage, call time, payment, and production requirements are agreed before the booking.
