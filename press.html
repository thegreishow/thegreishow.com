The Grei Show | Artist • Producer • Creative Director

Artist Profile

The Grei Show is a Jamaican recording artist, producer, engineer and creative visionary from Kingston, Jamaica. His sound blends reggae, dancehall, hip hop and global influences while staying rooted in Jamaican culture.

As the founder of Wheel It! Records, The Grei Show continues to build a creative ecosystem combining music, visuals, production and technology.
