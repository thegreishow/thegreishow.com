# Revenue Launch Checklist

The website now has three honest revenue paths: the published ebook, direct music support, and qualified creative-service inquiries. Daily revenue is a goal to measure and improve, not a guarantee.

## Live Now

- **Book:** Homepage and Books page send readers to the official *Astral Thread* ebook listing.
- **Music:** Homepage and Music page route listeners to Bandcamp and the release smart link.
- **Services:** Homepage and Connect page qualify production, creative direction, artist development, sync, performance, press, and partnership leads.

## Owner Setup Required

1. **Verify the book destination:** Replace the Amazon India URL with a global or territory-aware author link if one is available.
2. **Create a paid entry offer:** Choose one clear service deposit or consultation, define its deliverables and refund terms, then create a hosted checkout link.
3. **Connect scheduling:** Add a booking page only after the deposit or qualification step so calls stay high-intent.
4. **Connect the release list:** Replace the current email-app signup with a confirmed-opt-in newsletter form and a short welcome sequence.
5. **Connect analytics:** Attach an analytics or tag-manager account to the existing `window.dataLayer` events.
6. **Publish policies:** Add privacy, service terms, refund terms, and affiliate disclosures before collecting payments or subscriber data.

## First Offer Test

Start with one offer that uses existing credibility and requires no inventory, such as a production review, creative-direction session, or project deposit. Define:

- The exact outcome and deliverables.
- Who it is and is not for.
- Price, turnaround time, revision limits, and availability.
- A hosted payment link and confirmation message.
- A follow-up process for buyers.

Do not add multiple unproven products at once. Measure qualified visits, offer clicks, prepared inquiries, purchases, delivery time, and refunds every week.
