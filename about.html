About The Grei Show

Gamel Reid, known as The Grei Show, is a Jamaican recording artist and producer whose music flows between reggae, hip-hop, and modern beats. His sound carries the heartbeat of Jamaica while pushing boundaries with fresh ideas and storytelling.

Music & Collaborations

The Grei Show has made waves with his own releases and high-profile collaborations. He has produced and worked alongside notable artists like Protoje, Bay-C, Big Sugar, Blvk H3ro, and Supa Dups, contributing to tracks that blend conscious reggae vibes with modern production.

He’s also known for embracing the culture around marijuana and the lifestyle it intersects with, reflecting this naturally in his music and brand identity. From beats to visuals, his work embodies a relaxed yet intentional energy that resonates with fans across genres.

Creative Vision

The Grei Show is more than the music; it’s a creative universe where production, performance, and visual storytelling converge. Every project reflects a commitment to quality, authenticity, and a uniquely Jamaican perspective filtered through global sounds.

Whether in the studio, on stage, or through visuals, The Grei Show invites listeners to be part of a journey that blends culture, sound, and the future.
