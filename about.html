About The Grei Show

Gamel Reid, known as The Grei Show, is a Jamaican recording artist and producer whose music flows between reggae, hip-hop, and modern beats.

Music & Collaborations

He has worked with artists such as Protoje, Bay-C, Big Sugar, Blvk H3ro, and Supa Dups.

Creative Vision

The Grei Show is a multidisciplinary creative universe blending sound, visuals, and storytelling into a unified artistic identity.
