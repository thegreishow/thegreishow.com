How publishing works

Applications stay private until approved.

  1. Talent sends a complete application.
  2. White Line reviews identity, media, quality and fit.
  3. An approved profile is added to the managed roster.
  4. Clients can browse it and request a booking.
  5. Casting calls are posted publicly and removed when closed.

This first-stage version keeps publishing controlled by you. It does not give applicants direct access to edit the live website.
