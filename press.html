Official Biography

The Grei Show is the artist and production identity of Gamel Reid, a Jamaican recording artist, songwriter and music producer whose work moves through reggae, dancehall, hip-hop and experimental Caribbean sound. Raised in Bull Bay and based in Kingston, he approaches music as both a performer and a world-builder, connecting records with visual art, storytelling and interactive media.

His catalogue spans solo releases, collaborative projects and production work for Jamaican artists. A major credit is Protoje's “Weed & Ting,” created with Supa Dups for the album In Search of Lost Time. Reggaeville's coverage identifies The Grei Show among the album's selected Jamaican producers and specifically notes his keyboard contribution to the track. He is also the founder of Wheel It! Records, the independent platform behind his releases and creative collaborations.
